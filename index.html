ABOUT US

Karan Kothari Jewellers Pvt Ltd is one of the best known and iconic jewellery manufacturing and selling organizations in Nagpur. Established more than half a century back in 1965, KKJ Jewellers has a long tradition of presenting discerning connoisseurs with the most exquisite and exclusive traditional as well as contemporary Indian jewellery.

We at KKJ Jewellers have brought together a team of the most talented craftsmen and artisans who specialize in the creation of the most breath-taking jewellery in Silver, Gold and Platinum, studded with glittering diamonds and gemstones, and casting a bewitching spell on you at first sight. To capture the essence of gemstones and ensure that they are of the highest quality, we also have trained Graduate Gemologists and Graduate Jewelers on our team, who have achieved their certifications from the Gemological Institute of America (GIA).

For us, everything is centered around our Circle of Trust and Ethos. Our business is based, not on earning the maximum profit, but on earning the trust and loyalty of you – our most precious customers, who are more precious to us than the precious metals and stones that we deal in. Our legacy has been built on this Circle of Trust and Ethos, a legacy that has kept generations of customers coming to us for all their jewellery needs.

We do not look at customers simply as people to earn business from. We consider them part of our team, which allows them to freely share suggestions on how we can improve our services, and our designs. As a result, every design creation by KKJ Jewellers is driven by inputs from our valued customers, taken and molded into gorgeous pieces of art by our supremely honed craftsmen.
